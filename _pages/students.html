Mentorship, to me, is gratitude put into practice.

I enjoy collaborating with students at all levels on open-ended research problems and helping them develop ideas into strong, independent projects. If you're interested in working together, reach out.

Office hours. I'm happy to chat about research with students and fellow researchers. I've set aside Fridays, 4:00–5:00 PM (PST) for this — please reach out by email. If that time absolutely doesn't work, I'm happy to find another.
